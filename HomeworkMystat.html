Linear Equations

A linear equaation that can be written in the form

ax+b=0

where a and b are real numbers and x is a variable.This form is sometimes called the standard form of a linear equation.
Note that most linear equations will not start off in this form. Also, the variable may or may not be an x so don't get too
loocked into always seeing an x there.

Quadratic Equation

The sdandart form of a quadic equation looks like this:

ax2+bx+c=0

Where a, b, c are known values and x is the variable or unkown (we don't know it yet). Also, a can't be 0.
